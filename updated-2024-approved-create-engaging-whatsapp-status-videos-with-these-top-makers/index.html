Compatibility: iOS

Price: $0.99

Compatibility: iOS

Price: Free

Compatibility: Android, iOS

This photo editing app offers much more than the Vignette tool, as its users can process images in both JPEG and RAW file formats. Compatibility: Android , iOS

Compatibility: iOS

Furthermore, you can use the lightning adjustments to balance the shadows and highlights or process colors on separate channels. The PhotoToaster app is a great asset to all photographers who take photos with their iPhones, not only because it lets them create vignettes in just a few taps, but because it enables them to edit their photos professionally.

Price: Free, but contains in-app purchases
